Air Jordan 1 x Off-White

Retro HIGH

Created by designer Virgil Abloh, the Air Jordan 1 x Off-White comes in the classic Chicago colorway. Some of the sneakers' half-finished features include a Swoosh connected to the shoe with visible blue stitching, 'Air' printed on the midsole, and an Off-White zip tie on the collar.The box comes deconstructed as it's been turned inside-out with the inside consisting of a black base and gold Jumpman logo, while the outside is plain cardboard with the words 'Jumpman' printed on it .
