"Jumpman Jack TR 'Bright Cactus"

JUMP LOW 1

The Jordan Jumpman Jack TR 'Bright Cactus' has a neon green canvas upper with off-white tumbled leather overlays. A contrasting black Swoosh matches the collar and tongue, which is stamped with a Jumpman 'Crown' logo. 'TS' is embroidered on the forefoot strap, while a signature Cactus Jack smiley graphic graces the heel area. A Phylon midsole works with a rubber outsole underfoot to complete the look.
